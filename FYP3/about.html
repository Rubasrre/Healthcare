Company Background


#KitaJagaKita is a campaign to identify and validate organizations that are
dedicated to assisting the most vulnerable people, regardless of their
background. After being approved by the #kitajagakita volunteers, this list will
be updated with proposals submitted by you. To handle the COVID-19
epidemic, the Movement Control Order went into effect on March 18th, 2020,
and is projected to run until March 31st 2020 14th April 2020 30th April 2020 3
May 2020 14th June 2021. While the order is highly needed, the two-week
period is having a negative impact on the welfare and livelihoods of a number
of groups, including taxi drivers, laborers, food stall and small stall operators,
and other workers who lack proper social protection.
